Coffee Revolution

Sheffield Students' Union fantastic coffee shop with various types of coffee, tea, broad selection of sandwiches and pastries.

Bar One

Burgers, beer, sport and pool. Everything one could ever need in one place. The go-to hangout spot for a lot of students.

Interval

A Cafe bar with great, affordable pizzas, pastas and salads. A bit more sophisticated, serving wide selection of wine and beer, with live music in the evenings.

New Leaf

A truly modern salad bar, when you can come in and create a wrap or salad of your own. Fresh, healthy and tasty at the same time.

Proper Pasty

Excellent Cornish Pasty prepared with care and love. Always freshly baked and delicious.

Pearls

This Bubble Tea offers a great range of flavours as well as bases. Nice casual place to go to an pick up a drink.

The View Deli

Probably the best choice for someone not fond of meat. Made for vegans, vegetarians and allergic to gluten.

Grill & Go

Burritos, salads, wraps. Students' favourite restaurant that provides cooked fresh and made to order meals for everybody.

Inox

This amazing restaurant, while being a bit more expensive and fancy than most other places in Students' Union, offers lots of light options, daily specials and full restuarant menu prepared by an award-winning team, led by chef Joe Berry.
