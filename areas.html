Welcome Desk

Here at the welcome desk you can find any information you want and get all the help you need.

Box Office

Box office is the place where you can get every ticket you want to all the university related events.

Link to official information: Box Office

Student Advice Centre

If you need any help or do not know what to do, do not be afraid to visit Student Advice Centre. They are ready to help with literally every problem you might encounter at University, whether it's legal problems with your visa or needing help with your finances they are always there for you.

Link to official information: Student Advice Centre

Job shop

Job shop is a very useful place if you need help with finding a suitable job or placement at University. Don't worry if you are scared of writing your CV and cover letter they will also help you with that!

SSID

The Student Service Information Desk provides all the administrative services so you can go there if you for example need confirmation letters, or you lost your U-Card.

Shops

There are three main shops in the SU:

  • Our Shop - food,coffee,stationery
  • Zero Waste Shop - ecological kitchenware and food without packaging
  • Sheffield Store - clothes with university logo

    • Link to official information: Sheffield Store

Study spaces

Students' Union also has some nice study and relaxing spaces.The rooms available for booking are very similar to the ones Diamond and IC have. But what you won't find anywhere else is the one and only music room, which allows serious musicians to professionaly practice their craft.
